"The road to success is paved with good intentions, your success, then, begins with HGBaquiran College."

Mission

HG Baquiran College is recognized by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) and the Department of Education (DepEd). The school mainly offers undergraduate programs in Teacher Education, Business Administration, Criminology, Information Technology, and Hotel and Restaurant Management.

Vision

Honorato Guzman Baquiran (HGB) College is a private educational institution in Tumauini, Isabela. Since its foundation in 2005, the school provides quality education that builds graduates who are competent, active, and adaptive in navigating problems in both the local and global communities.
